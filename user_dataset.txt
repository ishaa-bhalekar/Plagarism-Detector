I am convinced that my cat, Sir Whiskerstein III, is secretly plotting to take over the world. You might think I am being dramatic, but allow me to explain. This is not your average loaf-of-bread-on-legs kind of cat. No, this feline is too intelligent for his own fur. Every morning, he sits by the window like a sophisticated villain surveying his empire. The squirrels tremble. The birds scatter. Even the mailman avoids eye contact. He does not meow - he issues commands. He does not purr - he vibrates with the power of manipulation. I once caught him watching a YouTube video on how to hypnotize humans using cuteness. I thought it was a joke. Until I found myself giving him my tuna sandwich and apologizing for not adding capers.He is got minions too - neighborhood cats that report to him during midnight meetings on the fence.
